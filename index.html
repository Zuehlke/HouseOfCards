House of cards

House of cards

House of cards is an educational application suite providing a common competition platform for arbitrary card games. Participants can create bots in their favourite programming language and compete with others in real time through HTTP.

Running games where your bot is battling can be viewed live in a browser which makes cheering more thrilling.

This documentation contains progress and important technical details.
